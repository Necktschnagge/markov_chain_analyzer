Takes markov chain object and creates transitions for herman's self-stabilizing algorithm.

Uses edge decoration (reward) at index 0 to store unique costs of 1 for each transition.

Parameters
mcThe markov chain object to modify, must be markov_chain::empty().
sizeThe size of the herman problem for which the states and transitions will be created. Must be odd and (_Integers << size) must not exceed limits of _Integers.
Exceptions
std::invalid_argumentMarkov chain must be empty.
std::invalid_argumentSize of herman must be odd.
std::invalid_argumentSize of herman is too big for storing all states in _Integers type.
std::invalid_argumentReward 0 needed for costs.
